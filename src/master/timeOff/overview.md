# Overview

The **Overview** section in the **Time-Off** plugin provides employees with a clear and organized view of their leave usage. It allows users to quickly see the leaves they have already taken, upcoming scheduled leaves, and any pending leave requests all in one place.

## Key Features

- Calendar View
- Displays all past, current, and future leave requests on a monthly calendar.
- Different colors represent different leave statuses for quick visibility.
- Helps employees plan their time-off effectively.

<ImagePopup src="/images1/time-off/overview.png" alt="Overview" />

## Click to View Leave Details

Click on any leave shown on the calendar to view details.

- A modal popup will open, showing:
- Leave Type (e.g., Sick Leave, Casual Leave, Earned Leave)
- Dates of leave (start & end)
- Reason for leave (if provided)
- Leave request status (Approved, Pending, Rejected)

<ImagePopup src="/images1/time-off/overview-view.png" alt="Leave Details" />

> Provides complete transparency and quick access to leave information.

## Summary

The **Overview** section offers an easy and intuitive way for employees to monitor their time-off usage. By presenting all leave information in a visual calendar layout, it saves time, reduces confusion, and helps employees stay informed about their approved, pending, and completed leave requests.
